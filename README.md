# AI-Powered Parametric Insurance for Gig Delivery Workers

**1. Problem Statement**

India’s gig economy relies heavily on delivery partners working for platforms such as Swiggy, Zomato, Amazon, and Zepto. These workers earn daily income based on completed deliveries. However, external disruptions such as extreme weather conditions, pollution, floods, and sudden curfews often prevent them from working.

Currently, gig workers do not have any financial protection against income loss caused by these uncontrollable external events. When disruptions occur, delivery partners lose a significant portion of their weekly earnings.

Our solution aims to build an **AI-powered parametric insurance platform** that automatically compensates delivery workers when such disruptions occur.


**2. Target Persona**

Name: Ravi Kumar

Challenges Faced

- Flooded roads stop deliveries
- Curfews or strikes restrict movement

These disruptions result in "loss of income for the worker".


**3. Proposed Solution**

We propose an **AI-driven parametric insurance platform** designed specifically for gig delivery workers.

The system provides **weekly insurance coverage** that protects delivery partners from income loss caused by environmental or social disruptions.

When a predefined disruption occurs, the system automatically triggers a claim and initiates payout without requiring manual claim submission.


**4. Application Workflow**

1. Delivery worker registers on the platform.
2. The system performs **AI-based risk profiling**.
3. A **weekly premium amount** is calculated based on risk factors.
4. Worker purchases an insurance policy.
5. The system continuously monitors disruption triggers using APIs.
6. If a disruption occurs, the **parametric trigger activates**.
7. Claim is automatically initiated.
8. The worker receives **instant payout for income loss**.


**5. Weekly Premium Model**

The insurance premium is calculated weekly based on location risk and historical disruption data.

 Risk Zone | Weekly Premium 

  Low Risk Area - ₹20 
  Medium Risk Area - ₹35 
  High Risk Area - ₹50 

Risk Factors Considered
- Historical weather data
- Flood-prone zones
- Pollution levels
- Traffic congestion

This dynamic model ensures affordable insurance for gig workers.


**6. Parametric Triggers**

The system automatically detects disruptions using predefined triggers.

 Disruption Type | Trigger Condition 

 Heavy Rain - Rainfall > 50mm 
 Extreme Heat - Temperature > 42°C 
 Severe Pollution - AQI > 300 
 Flood Alerts - Government flood warnings 
 Curfew - Local authority restrictions 

Once these conditions are met, the "claim process is automatically triggered".
